## 🔐 Strategy: The Verb Cipher Protocol
> **Protocol Document:** `Phase_1_Roadmapping/RoadmapAnalysis/Strategic_Blueprints/01_Protocol_The_Verb_Cipher.md`

**Core Concept:** We do not study "topics." We study "cognitive requirements" based on the Exam Objective's Command Verb.

| Level | Verbs | Goal | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **🟢 Level 1** | Explain, Compare, Summarize | Knowledge | Flashcards Only. No Lab. |
| **🟡 Level 2** | Configure, Implement, Use | Application | Build Lab. Know Syntax/Clicks. |
| **🔴 Level 3** | Troubleshoot, Analyze | Analysis | **Break-Fix Scenarios.** (24% of Exam) |
